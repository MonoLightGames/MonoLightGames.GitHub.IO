Dear partners,

Three years ago today, we naively founded MonoLight Games to develop games we love.

Similar to many small and medium size teams in Turkey, we ended up having lots of blockers.
At that point, we decided to become an enabler and empower others to plan, build and grow.

Since then, we had many opportunities to work with some of the best industry professionals.
We generously shared our network with partners to find best fit talents for their projects.
We supported a few promising ideas by advising with our existing know-how and expertise.
We accepted bunch of exciting challenges and delivered the best possible solutions in time.

As a small team of 7 like-minded individuals, it was an unforgettable experience so far.
Today, we believe mobile game development industry in Turkey is thriving like never before.
We are honored to be a part of it and very proud of our positive impact.

We can never thank you all enough for your vision and trust.
It is extremely hard for us to say goodbye but, it's time to change.

Sincerely,
MonoLight Games Team
